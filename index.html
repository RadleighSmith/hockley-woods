Essex's most enchanting woodland

Welcome to Hockley Woods, Where Nature's Magic Unfolds and Adventure Awaits!


Nestled in the heart of Essex. And one of the most enchanting woodlands in the area, Hockley Woods offers a serene and captivating escape for nature lovers and outdoor enthusiasts. With its ancient trees, diverse wildlife, and rich history, this woodland is a haven for exploration and tranquillity.

Immerse yourself in the beauty of Hockley Woods as you wander along well-marked trails, surrounded by towering trees and a symphony of birdsong. Discover seasonal wonders of the woods, from the vibrant bluebells carpeting the ground in the spring to the breathtaking hues of autumn foliage.


Sprawling across more than 130 hectares, stand as a remarkable testament to the past. These woods are a vestige of the vast wild woodlands that once blanketed Essex in the aftermath of the Ice Age, dating back 10,000 years.


Whether you're seeking a peaceful walk, a family picnic at the park, a bike ride, or even just a chance to reconnect with nature, Hockley Woods is the perfect destination.

How to find us!


Address:

Hockley Woods

Hockley,

SS5 4RQ


Directions by Car:

From the A127 Southend Arterial Road, take the exit towards Hockley/Rayleigh. Just after the Bull Pub there is a turning on the right which will take you into the car park.


Public Transport:

Hockley Woods is well-served by public transport. The closet train station is Hockley Station, which is just a short walk away from the woods. Various bus routes also provide easy access to the area.

Essex's most enchanting woodland

Welcome to Hockley Woods, Where Nature's Magic Unfolds and Adventure Awaits!


Nestled in the heart of Essex. And one of the most enchanting woodlands in the area, Hockley Woods offers a serene and captivating escape for nature lovers and outdoor enthusiasts. With its ancient trees, diverse wildlife, and rich history, this woodland is a haven for exploration and tranquility.

Immerse yourself in the beauty of Hockley Woods as you wander along well-marked trails, surrounded by towering trees and a symphony of birdsong. Discover seasonal wonders of the woods, from the vibrant bluebells carpeting the ground in the spring to the breathtaking hues of autumn foliage.


Covering over 130 hectares, Hockley Woods are the largest remaining area of the wild wood, which covered Essex after the Ice Age 10,000 years ago.


Whether you're seeking a peaceful walk, a family picnic at the park, bike ride, or even just a chance to reconnect with nature, Hockley Woods is the perfect destination.
